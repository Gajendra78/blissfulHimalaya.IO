Mallikarjun Mahadev Mandir Askote

Mallikarjun Mandir Askote

(11 Days / 10 Night)

Previous Next

There is such a Shivling in Pithoragarh in Uttarakhand, on which the milch cow used to offer its own milk. One of the two Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, this Shivling is known as Sri Mallikarjuna.

One of the two Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva is known as Sri Mallikarjuna. It is second in the Jyotirlingas. Mallika means Mother Parvati and Arjuna means Shiva. The Sri Mallikarjuna Mahadev temple situated on the hill of Ascot of Agnok is a part of it. This religious place is the center of faith not only of the people of the border region of India, but also of the people of neighboring country Nepal.

One of them milch cow used to go to a place secretly and offer its milk on its own. Gawale, who went with the cows, looked at him one day and was surprised. He saw that there was a self-effulgent Shivling, on which the cow was offering milk.

Through Gwale, when the news of this amazing incident came to the people living in the surrounding villages, the people got excited to see the Shivling. People started worshiping at this place since then. It is believed that a person gets relief from all his sufferings with the mere worship of this Shivling. Worshiping Mallikarjuna brings child and all happiness.
